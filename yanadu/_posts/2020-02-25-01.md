# Speaking using 'can'
>Increse a word order sense.

## Learning contents
'Can' is used to promise or guess.
You can use it depend on situations
When a sentence is negative, it accent 'can't',  otherwise accent a verb

## Examples
1. I can do it 
: 난 할수있어.
2. Can I come in? 
: 들어가도돼?
3. I can cry 
: 울수도있어
4. You can't park here
: 주차할수없어
