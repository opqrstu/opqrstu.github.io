>Speaking sentence with feeling.

## Learning contents
'will' has not only mean doing something in the future. It has a mean of a question. It depend on accent of speaking 

## Examples
1. Will you merry me ? 
: 나랑결해줄래?
2. Will he merry me? 
: 나랑결혼해줄까?
3. I will lose weight 
: 살뺄거야
4. I'll go 
: 갈게
