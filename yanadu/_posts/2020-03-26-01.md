## Learning contents

It is have to memory the past tense forms of words.

## Examples

1. I took it 
: 나 그거 가져갔어
2. I bought it 
: 나 그거 샀어
3. I brought it 
: 나 그거 가져왔어
4. I saw it 
: 나 그거 봤어
5. He was angry 
: 걔 화났어
6. People were angry 
: 사람들 화났어
