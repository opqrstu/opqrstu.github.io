>Speaking long and specific sentence .

## Learning contents

'which + pp' is used to express status of things.

'pp' is a passive action.

## Examples

1. A dog slept on the street is mine.
: 길에서 자는 개는 나의것이다.
2. I like a car broken by a ball.
: 나는 공에의해 부서진 차를 좋아한다.
