>Speaking sentence with feeling.

## Learning contents

'have' is a generel verb. 'have' has many meannings such as "먹다 가지다 낳다 하다 시간 겪다 등"

## Examples

1. I had lunch 
: 점심먹었어
2. I had water 
: 물 가졌어
3. I had a baby 
: 아이 낳았어
4. I had a walk 
: 산책했어
5. I had a good time 
: 좋은 시간이었어
6. I had an accident 
: 사고났어
