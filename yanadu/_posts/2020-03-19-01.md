>Speaking sentence with feeling.

## Learning contents

'get' is a generel verb. 'get' has many meannings such as "소유 받다 가져오다 되다 도착하다"

## Examples
1. I got a message 
: 문자받았어
2. I got a car 
: 나 차있어
3. I got to korea 
: 한국에 도착했어
4. Get a chair 
: 의자가져와
5. I got tired 
: 피곤해
6. I got the door 
: 문열었어
